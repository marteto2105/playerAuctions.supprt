Account Delivery Instructions

Please follow the steps below to complete your order delivery quickly and securely.

  1. Go to account.riotgames.com ↗
  2. Log in with your account details to access your Riot Account Dashboard.
  3. Navigate to Personal Information > Email Address and replace the existing email with the one below.
  4. Click Save and Verify to confirm the change.
  5. Notify the buyer when the change is completed. Please send a short message in the order chat confirming that the email was updated and verified.
